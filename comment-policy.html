Dolog Comment Policy Dolog aims to foster a respectful and engaging community. Please follow the guidelines below to maintain a welcoming environment for all users: Courtesy and Respect All comments should be written with respect towards other users. Inappropriate language, insults, or offensive behavior are not permitted. No Profanity or Hate Speech Comments containing profanity or hate speech will be subject to removal and potential sanctions. Relevant and Constructive Contributions Comments should be relevant to the post and contribute constructively to the discussion. Spamming or posting unrelated content is not allowed. Moderation Rights We reserve the right to moderate, edit, or remove comments that violate these policies. Thank you for reviewing and following our comment policy before posting!
